Heyo!

Hearthstone-OS is a project that I started in my free time. I've always wondered what it would be like to mod hearthstone. I couldn't figure it out, so I just made my own copy!

Can I play it?

You can download the repo and compile it yourself. Hearthstone-OS is made in Java, and the current git holds the eclipse configs. I'll create an actual build once I migrate off the console.

Is this allowed?

I have no idea. Blizzard owns every right to contact me and take this down if they want to. Hopefully they don't. I take no credit for the creation of the ideas in Hearthstone.
